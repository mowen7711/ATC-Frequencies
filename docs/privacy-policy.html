ATC Frequencies

Privacy Policy

Summary: ATC Frequencies does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Location access is used only on your device to show nearby airports and is never transmitted anywhere.

1. About This App

ATC Frequencies is published by 4T Technologies. The app provides worldwide airport ATC frequency information for general aviation reference. It does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use.

2. Location Data

The app requests access to your device's location for two purposes:

  • Nearby airports — to show airports close to your current position.
  • Signal reception estimate — to calculate approximate VHF radio range from a selected airport to your position.

If you enable the background airport monitor, location is also accessed while the app is in the background so the persistent notification can update with your nearest airports.

Your location is never transmitted to any server, stored beyond the current session, or shared with any third party. It is used solely on your device, in real time.

3. Airport and Frequency Data

Airport, frequency, runway, and navigation aid data is sourced from OurAirports.com (public domain / CC0). This data is downloaded directly to your device on first launch and refreshed periodically. No personal information is involved in this process.

4. Data Storage

All data stored by the app remains on your device only:

  • Airport database (downloaded from OurAirports)
  • Your starred (favourite) airports
  • Your home airport selection
  • App settings and preferences

None of this data leaves your device.

5. Anonymous Usage Metrics

The app collects anonymous, non-personal usage data to help improve the product. Specifically:

  • A randomly generated install identifier (UUID) — created on first launch, stored only on your device. It is not linked to your name, email, Google account, or any other identity.
  • Your device's language/region setting (e.g. "en-GB") — used to understand which languages are most common.
  • App version number.
  • Events such as app opened, screen viewed, airport viewed, frequency copied, and session duration.

This data is sent to a Cloudflare Worker relay, which automatically adds an approximate country and city derived from your IP address (the IP address itself is never stored). Events are then written to a private database operated by 4T Technologies.

What is not collected: your name, email, precise GPS location, device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID), or any information that could identify you as an individual. The app does not use advertising SDKs or share data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Analytics are disabled entirely when running a development build of the app.

6. Third-Party Services

The app connects to the following external services:

  • OurAirports.com — downloads airport and frequency data on first launch and weekly thereafter. No personal data is sent.
  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles loaded when viewing an airport map. Standard tile request; no personal data beyond a routine IP connection.
  • Cloudflare Workers — anonymous metrics relay (see Section 5).
  • LiveATC.net — optional link-out to live ATC audio streams, opened in your browser. Subject to LiveATC's own privacy policy.

The app does not integrate advertising networks, social login, or tracking SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13, or any other age. No personal data is collected from any user.
