What it does

ATC Frequencies is an Android app that puts every airport's air traffic control frequencies in your pocket. Search by name, ICAO, IATA code or city, then tap through to tower, ground, approach, ATIS and clearance frequencies — plus runways, navaids, and a physics-based estimate of whether you'll actually receive the signal from where you're standing. No account, no API key, all offline once the data's downloaded.

Will you actually hear it?

Every airport detail screen runs a VHF line-of-sight calculation — using your GPS altitude, the airport's elevation, and a 4/3 Earth radius refraction model — to estimate signal reception before you even tune in.

Out of practical range? The app tells you, and points you to a live audio stream on LiveATC.net instead.

This is a calculation, not a guarantee. Terrain, buildings and weather all affect real-world reception, and your actual results will vary with the specific radio and antenna you're using.
